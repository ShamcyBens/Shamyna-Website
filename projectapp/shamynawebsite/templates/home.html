How We Met

It all began with a simple message, a message that changed our lives forever.

I still remember the day vividly, I was seeking information about the 1st-year orientation, and as fate would have it, I stumbled upon a conversation where your name appeared. Little did I know that sending that message would lead me to you, the love of my life.

Despite the initial confusion and misunderstanding, every moment was worth it because it brought me closer to you. Two years of waiting, dreaming, and hoping finally led to the most beautiful chapter of our lives.

You, with your unwavering faith, values, and kindness, captivated my heart like no other. Every day spent getting to know you felt like a blessing, and I knew deep within my soul that you were meant to be mine.

From our shared laughter to our heartfelt conversations, every memory we've created together has only strengthened our bond. Our love story is a testament to the power of fate and the beauty of finding true love when least expected.

Today, as we embark on this journey together, hand in hand, I am grateful for every twist and turn that led me to you. You are my soulmate, my partner, and my greatest love, and I cherish every moment we share.
